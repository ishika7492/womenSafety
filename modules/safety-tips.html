General Safety Tips

  • Stay Aware of Your Surroundings: Always be mindful of your environment. Avoid distractions like looking at your phone while walking.
  • Trust Your Instincts: If something feels off, trust your gut feelings and take action to protect yourself.
  • Keep Your Personal Items Secure: Use a crossbody bag and keep it close to you. Be cautious of pickpockets in crowded areas.
  • Have a Safety Plan: Share your whereabouts with trusted friends or family, and establish a plan for different scenarios.
  • Learn Self-Defense: Consider taking a self-defense class to build confidence and learn techniques for protecting yourself.
